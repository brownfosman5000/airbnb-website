About Us

Who we are and what we are about

Sunrise Element brings our guests a extraordinary travel experience. We offer apartments, lofts, and vacation homes to professional travelers in the most popular neighborhoods. Some of the locations are just walking distance away to the some of the most stunning attractions.

Our rentals provide top quality amenities, such as travel-size toiletries, towels, & premium linen for every stay. In addition, we provide more of a home away from home experience with spacious kitchens and comfortable living spaces.

Our guests find comfort, quality, and safety at every stay. Our local teams at each location work hard to maintain a consistent quality experience for each guest. Sunrise Element is available 24/7 via text or phone call to answer any questions, concerns, or accommodations.

Do you plan to stay with us for a month or even longer? Well, you're in the right spot. Sunrise Element offers 30+ night stays to accommodate the corporate/business traveler, long-haul vacationer, and digital nomad. Located in Philidelphia, we offer travelers extended stay accommodations within our fully-furnished apartment suites that seamlessly integrate work and play while providing all the comforts of home.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Does Sunrise Element screen their guests?

Yes. No matter the length of stay, we require identity verification and screening before a reservation.

How does Sunrise Element market its suites?

We advertise our suites here on our direct booking website. We also market them on other third-party short-term rental sites.

Who are Sunrise Element guests, and how long are their typical stay

Sunrise Element guests include those traveling for leisure, business, or corporate stays. Our stays range from one night to six months, with an average stay of three nights.

Does Sunrise Element have a cleaning team?

After each stay, our professional cleaning team will inspect and clean each unit. If a guest stays for an extended period, we clean the apartment weekly upon request.
